Welcome to Darbhanga

Darbhanga, located in the Mithila region of Bihar, is one of Indiaâ€™s cultural treasures. It is a place where tradition meets heritage, and where the past resonates through its music, architecture, and local customs.

Known for its rich Maithili culture and the royal lineage of the Darbhanga Raj, the city has been a center of art, education, and music for centuries. The grand Darbhanga Palace and Raj Qila are testaments to its historical significance.

The people of Darbhanga are proud torchbearers of their language, Maithili, and celebrate festivals like Chhath, Holi, and Diwali with immense joy. The city is also home to educational institutions like Lalit Narayan Mithila University.

Whether you're walking through its historic streets or enjoying classical dhrupad music, Darbhanga offers a unique blend of beauty, knowledge, and tradition.
